Start With Basics

In this online course, mentors work with you in 1-on-1 mentor sessions on the basics of web software development, startup culture (Lean startup), and business planning. You will learn the basics and then move on to advanced topics and techniques like pair programming, and a bit more about the mechanics of working with a startup team. You set the pace of the course with a total course duration ranging from 4 weeks at 3 hours per day to 10 weeks at 1 hour per day.

Technologies taught include Elm, GraphQL, and Dark
Duration: ~ 4-10 weeks

Create Your MVP

Once you have completed the Start With Basics course, you progress to this course where you learn the techniques needed to build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP). The MVP is the first version of your business idea, and it is developed through 1-on-1 mentor sessions using Lean Startup and Agile Product development skills. At the conclusion of this course, your MVP will be ready to be tested by customers.
Duration: ~ 8-12 weeks
