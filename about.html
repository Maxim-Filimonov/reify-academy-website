Our Vision

When we started Reify Academy, our vision was to make it easier for non-technical entrepreneurs to start businesses with models that require custom software. We had the inspiration that a non-technical entrepreneur is best served by learning enough technical know-how to start a successful software-based business without the middlemen.  We aim to do just that. Our team has a unique experience of both traditional coding boot camps mentors and students with backgrounds in business. The combination of which helps us see software development and business problems from multiple perspectives.
